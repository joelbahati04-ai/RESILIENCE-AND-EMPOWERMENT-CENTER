Become A Volunteer Walk With Us To Empower And Heal

the Resilience and Empowerment Center, we are building more than programs we are nurturing a movement of healing, dignity, and hope for refugees and vulnerable families in the Nakivale Refugee Settlement. We are calling on passionate individuals both local and international to join us as volunteers and become part of this life changing journey. Our Vision in Action We envision a community where every person, regardless of their background or past wounds, can access holistic healing, live in peace, and regain their human dignity. Through emotional support, empowerment programs, and trauma recovery tools, we help people not just survive but thrive.
Our Core Values Drive Everything We Do: Integrity We serve with honesty, accountability, and transparency. Collaboration We believe change happens through teamwork and unity. Compassion We walk with others in love, empathy, and care. Empowerment We give people tools to rebuild their lives and futures.
